My Dumb App

  • Outline of canvas object in yellow, background color of canvas is white. This is controlled from the HTML level.
  • The root interactor bridges the HTML and tropical worlds. It shifts its content inward by 5 pix, paints background melon colored and rounds its corners
  • DumbParent fills its upper left quadrant with cornsilk (sort of brownish) as well as showing its border in same color. Note that it tries to just do a simple filled rectangle but since its parent (the root interactor) set the clipping path with rounded rects, the upper left remains rounded
  • Blue rectangles (royal blue and dodger blue) are instances of DumbChild. They just fill their entiring bounding rect with a color.
  • The red circle is actually a composite. The Portole type masks its child, whatever it is, with an arc. We use a the same DumbLeaf as before, but the masking removes the part outside the boundary of Porthole.
  • The blue squares and red circle respond clicking by switching from filled to stroked. Note that the clipping rect of the porthole on the round object is pretty clearly shown.
  • The clickable items respond to mouse focus in the sense that they only perform their switch if you click and release within their bounds.
